Welcome to lansly! You can learn with lansly app, Android app, Here’s a helpful guide on how to use lansly:
As you progress through your lessons, YOU FOLLOW THE STEPS ONE BY ONE RESPECTING TIMING AND NUMBER OF ATTEMPTS THAT ARE MENTIONED IN THE APP. THE STEPS AS BELOW
1- LISTEN TO THE STORY 3 TIMES
2- READ THE STORY LOUDLY 3 TIMES
3- READ THE TRANSLATION OF THE STORY
4- LISTEN TO THE STORY 3 TIMES
5- RECORD THE STORY BY YOUR VOICE 3 TIMES
6- REPEAT STEPS 4 AND 5 THREE TIMES
7- EXPRESS IMAGES OF THE STORY
8- COMPARE YOUR WRITING WITH THE STORY
